Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. GemEntry is committed to protecting your data and maintaining transparency about how we use it.

We may collect basic user information, such as:

  • Device information (for app performance monitoring)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data only to:

  • Provide and personalize the app experience
  • Store and calculate polishing records accurately

4. Third-Party Services

GemEntry may use third-party tools (e.g., Google AdMob) to serve ads and monitor performance. These tools may collect anonymized usage data and device insights.

5. Data Security

We take your security seriously. All sensitive data is stored locally or encrypted. We do not share user data with third parties beyond services mentioned above.

6. Your Rights

You may request to view, edit, or delete your data anytime. Visit your Profile settings in the app or contact support for assistance.

7. Updates

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in law or features. Any major changes will be notified in-app.
