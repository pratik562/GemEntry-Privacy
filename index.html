Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. GemEntry is committed to protecting your data and maintaining transparency about how we use it.

2. What We Collect

We may collect basic user information, such as:

  • Phone number (for OTP login)
  • Selected language & preferences
  • Work price entries & history (stored securely)
  • Device information (for app performance monitoring)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data only to:

  • Provide and personalize the app experience
  • Store and calculate polishing records accurately
  • Improve feature performance and support

4. Third-Party Services

GemEntry may use third-party tools for services like analytics and ads (e.g., Google AdMob), which may collect anonymous usage data for personalization and reporting.

5. Data Security

We use modern security practices to protect your information. All sensitive entries are stored locally or securely encrypted with no public access.

6. Your Rights

You may request to edit or delete your data at any time. Simply go to your Profile in the app or contact our support team.

7. Updates

This policy may be updated in the future. We’ll notify you in-app of any material changes.
